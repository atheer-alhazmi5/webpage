chocolate chip cookies recipe

INGREDIENTS

  1. 2 ¼ cups all purpose flour
  2. 1tsp baking soda
  3. 1tsp salt
  4. 200g butter,softened
  5. ¾ cup granulated suger
  6. ¾ cup brown suger
  7. 1 tsb vanilla extract
  8. 2 large eggs
  9. cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate
    chips

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. preheat oven to 180℃
  2. combine flour, baking soda &
    salt in small bowl.
  3. beat butter, sugar& vanilla in
    a mixer bowl until creamy.
  4. add eggs, one at a time
  5. gradually add flour mixture
  6. add chocolate chips and mix
  7. bake for 9 to 11 minutes
  8. enjoy !
